This lsystems folder needs to be within the examples folder of octet.

Controls:
1-8 - change between trees/curves
Left and Right Arrows - change the angle
Up and Down Arrows - change the iteration of the tree
W and S - increase and decrease the length of the line
U and O - zoom in and zoom out
I and K - move camera up and down
J and L - move camera left and right
